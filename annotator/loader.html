🎓 Annotate

How to install

  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B)
  2. Drag the green button above to your bookmarks bar
  3. Navigate to any page (experiment, article, student work)
  4. Click the bookmarklet to activate the tools
  5. Press M for mouse, L for laser, H for highlighter, E for eraser, B for box, Shift for pan
  6. Hold Ctrl + scroll to zoom the entire page
  7. Click the 📷 button to save a screenshot

Annotations save locally per page. No data is sent anywhere.
