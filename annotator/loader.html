🎓 Annotate

📋 Installation

  1. Show your bookmarks bar (Ctrl+Shift+B)
  2. Drag the green button above to your bookmarks bar
  3. Navigate to any page and click the bookmarklet

🎨 Tools

P Pen   S Select   G Pan   T Text   K Black Box   H Highlighter   E Eraser   B Box Outline

1-6 Switch colors   Space Show/Hide overlay

Ctrl+Scroll Brush size   Ctrl+Z Undo

⬛ Black Boxes

Right-click a black box to remove it individually.

Press Esc (while Blackout tool is active) to clear all black boxes at once.

Drag any black box to reposition it over hidden content.

Annotations save locally per page. No data sent anywhere.
